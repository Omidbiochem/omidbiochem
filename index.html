About

Ph.D. Candidate in Clinical Biochemistry | Researcher in Systems Biology and Computational Approaches I am currently pursuing a Ph.D. in Clinical Biochemistry at Iran University of Medical Sciences. I completed my B.Sc. in Biology at Shahid Beheshti University in Tehran, and my M.Sc. in Clinical Biochemistry at Tabriz University of Medical Sciences. My main academic interests include bioinformatics, computational biology, biological modeling, and neurodegenerative diseases. In recent years, I have expanded my research focus by incorporating emerging technologies, artificial intelligence, and computational tools into the analysis of biological data. I firmly believe that the future of medicine lies in the integration of life sciences with computational power. Currently, I am exploring the field of male infertility, conducting a comprehensive review of the scientific literature related to its diagnosis and treatment. My long-term vision is to establish an innovative center dedicated to the development of intelligent, biochemically driven diagnostic and therapeutic strategies for infertility. I am passionate about innovative thinking, interdisciplinary collaboration, and the translation of scientific knowledge into practical and clinical solutions. .
